Presidents of Botswana

  • Sir Seretse Khama

    Botswana's founding president, led the country from independence in 1966 until 1980.
  • Quett Masire

    President from 1980 to 1998, he was known for promoting education and democracy.
  • Festus Mogae

    Served from 1998 to 2008, focusing on economic growth and HIV/AIDS awareness.
  • Ian Khama

    Led from 2008 to 2018, emphasizing discipline and infrastructure development.
  • Mokgweetsi Masisi

    President from 2018 to 2024, focusing on education reform and innovation.
  • Duma Boko

    Current president since 2024, bringing progressive leadership and inclusive governance.
