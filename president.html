Presidents of Botswana

Sir Seretse Khama
Botswana's founding president, led the country from independence in 1966 until 1980.

Quett Masire
President from 1980 to 1998, known for promoting education and democracy.

Festus Mogae
Served from 1998 to 2008, focusing on economic growth and HIV/AIDS awareness.

Ian Khama
Led from 2008 to 2018, emphasizing discipline and infrastructure development.

Mokgweetsi Masisi
President from 2018, focusing on education reform and innovation.

Duma Boko
The current president, known for his legal background and bold leadership approach.He has promised that he would bring about change with the tetiary level student allowance.
