Athletics Achievements

Isaac Makwala – One of Africa’s fastest sprinters, Makwala represented Botswana with distinction on the world stage. He famously ran alone on the track at the 2017 World Championships to qualify for the finals after being quarantined.

Letsile Tebogo – A rising star in athletics, Tebogo became the first Botswanan athlete to win a gold medal at the Olympics in the 200m race during the 2024 Paris Olympics, defeating world champion Noah Lyles.

Relay Team – Botswana’s 4x400m men’s relay team made history by winning a bronze medal at the Tokyo 2020 Olympics and followed it with a silver medal win at the 2024 Paris Olympics, showing consistency and determination on the global stage.
